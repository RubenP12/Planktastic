News & Events

Follow Planktastic's progress — field campaigns, milestones and stakeholder events

Project Launch · January 2025

Planktastic Kick-off Meeting

Planktastic officially launched with a kick-off meeting bringing together the CIIMAR and INESC TEC teams to review the work plan and align on project goals for 2025–2027.

🌊
Field Work · Spring 2025

First Field Campaigns at Douro & Lima Estuaries

Seasonal sampling begins at the Douro estuary in Porto and the Lima estuary in Viana do Castelo, collecting the first plankton tows and microplastics samples of the project.

📣
Engagement · September 2025

First Stakeholder Workshop (M2)

Environmental managers, fisheries experts and NGOs gathered for Planktastic's first stakeholder workshop, exploring how the project can support real-world monitoring and policy decisions.
